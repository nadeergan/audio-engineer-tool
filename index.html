Audio Engineer Tool – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2024

Audio Engineer Tool ("the App") is a free utility application designed for sound engineers, musicians, and vocal artists. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data to ensure transparency and trust.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. The App operates as an "offline-first" tool. No personal data (such as names, emails, or phone numbers) is requested, required, or transmitted to the developer.

2. Permissions & Feature Usage

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)
    Purpose: To provide core functionality including the Tuner, Pitch Detection, and Audio Recorder.
    Usage: Audio input is processed instantly and locally on your device.
    Privacy: No audio data is uploaded to any external server. All processing happens in real-time within the App's memory or saved directly to your device's local storage at your request.
  • Storage (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
    Purpose: To save your recordings and allow you to access, rename, delete, or share them.
    Usage: The App accesses only the files it creates or files you explicitly select for playback.
    Privacy: Your recordings remain on your device. We do not have access to your files.

3. Advertising

This App is supported by Google AdMob to keep it free for everyone.

AdMob may collect abstract technical data (e.g., device type, advertising ID) to serve relevant non-personalized or personalized ads. This data collection is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. No User Registration

The App requires no login, no account creation, and no password. We do not maintain any user database.

5. Contact Us
