Audio Engineer Tool – Privacy Policy

Audio Engineer Tool is a free application for sound engineers and vocal artists.

Data Collection

The app does not collect personal user data. Audio processing is performed locally on the device.

Microphone Access

Microphone access is required only for the tuner feature and is processed in real time without recording or transmitting audio.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Permissions

  • RECORD_AUDIO – used for tuner functionality only
  • INTERNET – required for loading ads
